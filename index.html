About Us

At Perception Sun and Sport Eyewear, we are compassionate about the rich life experience that people create through their own unique perspective. While Sunglasses are an essential Florida accessory, we are also fans of art and design and draw inspiration from you. Our store is bursting with the latest high quality designer styles as well as the leading performance eyewear available for sports enthusiasts. Perception Sun and Sport is recognized as a leader in specialty sun retail and you will only receive 100% authentic frames featuring brands such as Maui Jim, Tom Ford, Ray-Ban, Prada, Oakley, Gucci, Spy, Rudy Project and so much more. As vision care advocates, we strive to enhance your shopping experience by maintaining a fun, personal visit to our store. Come celebrate your style with us and add a hint of sophistication to your outfit or a dash of glamour to your afternoon. Whether you are seeking lenses based on fashion, performance, or lifestyle the most important factor is linking your individuality to the proper protection for healthy eyes. Perception Sun and Sport will provide you with just what you are looking for at prices that are made in the shade.
